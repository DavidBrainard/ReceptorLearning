brainardlab.nben.retina.constants documentation

CIE-xyz1931-matrix
Vector of three vectors which makeup the spectral sensitivities of the CIE 1931 color matching
functions; this was grabbed from the Psychtoolbox's T_xyz1931.mat file, but contains only the
values sampled at 400:10:720/(range 400 720 10).
spectral-sensitivities-matrix
Map of spectral sensitivities, which maps a wavelength in the range of 400-580 (inclusive) to a
spectral sensitivity vector sampled at wavelengths (range 400 721 10)
